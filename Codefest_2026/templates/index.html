CO₂ Emissions Explained

CO₂ emissions are produced when carbon-based fuels are burned. More driving and fuel use increases your environmental impact. Petrol and diesel cars produce high CO₂, while hybrid and electric vehicles reduce emissions. Electric vehicles are cleaner on the road but consider battery production impacts as well.
